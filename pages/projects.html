The Stage Manager's Book
During Spring 2024, I collaborated with four other students at The University of Alabama to develop an application for theater management. After creating their profile, a user can create a hub for their production and invite others to join. Within this hub, the stage manager can upload and annotate a script, post announcements, and share production files. We built this application using Node.js, React, and MongoDB.
View The Stage Manager's Book repository
Pronoun Lists in Profile Bios
Durig Summer 2022, I conducted research into characteristics of Twitter users with a pronoun list (e.g. she/her, they/shem) in their profile bio. I wrote Python scripts to access data from SQL databases and the Twitter API and visualized results in R. I am the lead author on a paper published from this research in the Journal of Quantitative Description.
